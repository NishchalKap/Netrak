# Netrak Landing Page

## Goal

Establish Netrak as public-interest infrastructure that is contemporary, calm, and credible to citizens, government, banks, and telecom partners.

## Page sequence

1. Header: Netrak mark, navigation, sign in, appearance control.
2. Hero: short mission statement, one primary call to action, restraint over illustration.
3. Proof: measurable impact and partner trust.
4. The problem: fraud harm described in human, not sensational, language.
5. How it works: detect, report, coordinate, recover.
6. Role journeys: citizen, officer, institution.
7. AI capability: explainable assistance, not magical claims.
8. Architecture: privacy, auditability, resilient integrations.
9. Footer: help, privacy, accessibility, service status.

## Hero copy pattern

Eyebrow: `DIGITAL PUBLIC SAFETY`

Headline: “A calmer way to stay protected.”

Supporting copy: one or two sentences, maximum 140 characters per paragraph.

Primary CTA: “Continue to Netrak” or “Report suspicious activity.”

## Landing responsive rules

- Mobile: one intentional message per viewport; no dense logo cloud.
- Desktop: 12-column layout, headline occupying 6–7 columns.
- Theme control remains in the top right and exposes its state to assistive technology.
